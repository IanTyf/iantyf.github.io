Ian is a game designer and gameplay programmer based in New York City.


His work focuses on exploring creative and unconventional gameplay interactions
and how they evoke different emotions and conversations.


He's currently an M.F.A student at NYU Game Center, and before he received his B.A in Mathematics and Comupter Science from Courant Institute at NYU. He also worked at Tencent Timi Studios on the Division: Resurgence, as well as Future Reality Lab under Professor Ken Perlin.


He plays too much Sudoku and holds a world record of Super Auto Pets speedrun.
