Ian is a game designer and gameplay programmer based in New York.


His work focuses on exploring creative and unconventional mechanics and gameplay interactions, and how they convey emotions and evoke conversations.

He believes games matter. In many ways.




currently an M.F.A student at NYU Game Center

previously B.A in Mathematics and Comupter Science from NYU Courant
